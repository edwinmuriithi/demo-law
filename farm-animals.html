Farming Systems

LAPA recognizes the vital connection between a healthy planet and a just food system. We advocate for policies and practices that ensure everyone has access to safe, nutritious food while protecting our environment. Through collaboration with policymakers, farmers, and consumers, we can create a future where food nourishes both people and the planet.

Our Methods:

• Legal Advocacy: We draft and advocate for legislation that promotes sustainable food systems.
• Litigation: We pursue legal cases that challenge unfair practices or environmental degradation within the food system.
• Community Education: We raise awareness about the importance of sustainable food systems and empower consumers to make informed choices.

Working Animals

LAPA fights for a future where working animals are treated with dignity. We address the inadequate legal framework surrounding animal welfare, breeding, transport, and exploitation through a comprehensive legal approach.

Our Strategies:

• Law Reform: We advocate for stronger animal welfare legislation at national and county levels.
• Policy Engagement: We work with policymakers to ensure animal welfare is prioritized.
• Litigation: We pursue criminal cases related to animal theft and use civil litigation to drive progressive legal change.

Join us in creating a future where legal protections translate to better lives for working animals.
