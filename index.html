Law. Science. Action.
Protecting Africa's Animals and Ecosystems

Our Approach

Strategic Litigation

We work in the courts to protect animals and the environment and use the law to challenge interests averse to animal interests and their status.

Policy & Advocacy

We support causes that advocate for the enhancement of the welfare and status of non–human animals, the habitats they live in and the communities that live with them. We expose, document and identify various animal rights, welfare, abuse and related concerns.

Science & Research

We conduct high-quality research to inform decisions, shape policy, and drive advocacy. Our data and insights help NGOs, governments, and the public understand root causes, evaluate program impacts, identify trends, and find solutions for communities and causes.

Legal Aid

We advise and offer legal assistance on regulatory, policy, and justice issues for nonprofits, communities and the public working with non-human animals. We support them to unlock and uphold their rights and navigate the complexities of the legal system.

Current Projects

Project WAVES

Project WAVES is a campaign for the protection of marine species and their habitats. We work for community members, with policymakers and in the courts to advance the protection of endangered marine species from illegal, unreported, and unregulated fishing.

CLAWS

Our Community Legal Aid Workers (CLAWS) provide legal assistance to communities affected by human-wildlife conflict and promote wildlife conservation. CLAWS are trained to provide legal support, raise legal awareness, and empower communities to navigate legal processes effectively.

Justice for Wildlife

We leverage laws and technology to disrupt criminal networks that trade in wildlife. We work with policymakers and law enforcement to strengthen their response to environmental crime.

EcoSyNC

Kenya's egg market is projected to grow by 211% by 2050, but challenges in welfare, sustainability, and safety persist. The EcoSustainable Nurture Certified program, via the EcoSyNC platform, will establish a certification process promoting ethical and sustainable production. This certification will enhance market value by endorsing One Health and One Welfare principles.
