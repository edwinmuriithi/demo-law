Law. Science. Action.

Securing Species and the Environment.

We believe the Law should work to secure species and the environment. We work in the courts, with communities and policy makers to transform, strengthen and enhance the law to secure and protect the environment and animal species.

“Law Should Work”

“One Health One Welfare”

We apply the One Health approach that recognizes interconnectedness of human health, animal health, and the environment. We fashion solutions that embrace this intersectionality through multi-disciplinary efforts.



Our Approach

Strategic Litigation

We work in the courts to protect animals and the environment.

Policy & Advocacy

We advocate for the enhancement of the welfare of animals, habitats they live in and the communities that live with them.

Science & Research

We research to drive informed decision-making, shaping policy, and advocating for change.

Legal Assistance

We advise and offer legal support on regulatory, policy, and justice issues.



Current Projects

Project WAVES

Project WAVES is a campaign for the conservation of marine species and protection of their habitats.

CLAWS

Community Legal Aid Workers (CLAWS) provide legal assistance to communities affected by human-wildlife conflict.

Justice for Wildlife

We leverage laws and technology to empower law enforcement and disrupt criminal networks that trade in wildlife.

Safeguarding Working Animals

We advocate for strengthened legal protections for working animals from cruelty and over exploitation.

EcoSyNC

We implement Kenya’s first independent certification program that verifies the eco - sustainability of farm production.
