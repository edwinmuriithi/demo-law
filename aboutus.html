About Us

Our Vision

• Animals, both wild and working, are treated with compassion and protected from exploitation.
• Wildlife populations thrive in healthy ecosystems, coexisting peacefully with human communities.
• The power of law is effectively used to create a just and sustainable world for animals and the environment.
• Sustainable food systems provide safe, nutritious food for all while safeguarding the environment for future generations.

Our Mission

• LAPA stands as a resolute champion, fighting for a future where animals are treated with respect and the environment flourishes in harmony. Their mission is clear: to promote animal welfare and environmental sustainability through legal advocacy and collaboration.

Values Statements

Collaboration forms the cornerstone of LAPA's approach. They believe that by working together with communities, policymakers, and other organizations, they can achieve far greater impact. Additionally, LAPA prioritizes:

• Empowerment: Equipping communities with the legal tools and knowledge they need to advocate for themselves and their environment.
• Research-driven Action: Informing their advocacy efforts with sound research to create data-driven solutions.
• Innovation: Continuously seeking new and effective legal strategies to address complex challenges.
• Integrity: Maintaining the highest ethical standards in their work.

• LAPA is more than just an organization; it's a movement. By embodying these values and relentlessly pursuing their mission and vision, LAPA strives to create a future where both animals and the environment can thrive.
